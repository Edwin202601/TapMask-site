TapMask Support

Help with TapMask for iPhone

Using TapMask

Select photos, take a photo, or send images to TapMask from the iOS Share sheet. Tap detected private information to mask it, then export the result to Photos, Files, or another app.

Purchases

TapMask Pro is a one-time, non-consumable purchase. If a previous purchase is not recognized, open TapMask Settings and choose Restore Purchases.

Troubleshooting

If camera or export access was denied, open iOS Settings, find TapMask, and review its permissions. Restart TapMask after changing permissions.

Contact

To report a problem, open an issue in the TapMask support tracker. Do not attach private or unmasked images. Include your iOS version, TapMask version, and steps to reproduce the problem.
